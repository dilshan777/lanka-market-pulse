# Dialog Axiata Announces Major 5G Expansion Plans for Sri Lanka

**Dialog Axiata PLC (DIAL.N0000)**, Sri Lanka's leading telecommunications provider, today announced ambitious plans to expand its 5G network coverage to **75% of the population** by the end of 2026, representing one of the largest infrastructure investments in the company's history.

## Investment Details

The company plans to invest approximately **LKR 15 billion** in 5G infrastructure over the next 18 months, including:

- **2,500 new 5G base stations** across the island
- **Fiber optic backbone expansion** to support 5G backhaul
- **Edge computing infrastructure** for low-latency applications
- **5G-enabled enterprise solutions** for B2B customers

## Coverage Targets

| Region | Current 5G Coverage | Target by Dec 2026 |
|--------|---------------------|---------------------|
| Western Province | 45% | 90% |
| Central Province | 20% | 70% |
| Southern Province | 25% | 75% |
| Northern & Eastern | 10% | 60% |
| Other Provinces | 15% | 65% |

## Market Impact

The announcement sent Dialog's shares up **2.40%** to close at LKR 12.80, with trading volume surging to **5.2 million shares**.

**Key benefits expected:**

1. **Revenue Growth**: 5G services expected to add LKR 3-4 billion in annual revenue by 2027
2. **ARPU Improvement**: Average revenue per user projected to increase by 15-20%
3. **Enterprise Solutions**: New revenue streams from IoT, smart cities, and industrial automation
4. **Competitive Advantage**: First-mover advantage in 5G enterprise services

## Technology Partnerships

Dialog has partnered with leading global technology providers:

- **Ericsson**: Radio access network equipment
- **Nokia**: Core network infrastructure
- **Huawei**: Fiber optic transmission systems
- **Cisco**: Enterprise networking solutions

## Government Support

The Telecommunications Regulatory Commission of Sri Lanka (TRCSL) has granted Dialog **additional spectrum** in the 3.5 GHz band to support the 5G rollout. The government has also agreed to:

- Expedite tower permit approvals
- Reduce regulatory fees for 5G infrastructure
- Support digital literacy programs
- Promote 5G adoption in public services

## Consumer Benefits

**For individual consumers:**
- Download speeds up to 1 Gbps
- Ultra-low latency for gaming and streaming
- Enhanced mobile broadband experience
- Improved network reliability

**For enterprises:**
- Private 5G networks for factories and warehouses
- IoT connectivity for smart agriculture
- Real-time remote monitoring solutions
- Cloud gaming and AR/VR applications

## Analyst Commentary

**Mr. Rajitha Silva, Telecom Analyst at Asia Securities:**
> "Dialog's 5G expansion is a game-changer for Sri Lanka's digital economy. The investment will not only benefit Dialog's shareholders but also accelerate digital transformation across multiple sectors. We maintain our 'BUY' rating with a target price of LKR 15.00."

## Competitive Landscape

Dialog's 5G push puts pressure on competitors:

- **Mobitel (SLT)**: Currently testing 5G in limited areas
- **Hutch**: Focused on 4G expansion in urban centers
- **Airtel**: Evaluating 5G readiness

## Conclusion

Dialog Axiata's 5G expansion represents a significant milestone in Sri Lanka's telecommunications sector. The investment is expected to drive long-term value creation while positioning Sri Lanka as a digital hub in the South Asian region.
