# ASPI Gains 45 Points as Banking Stocks Lead Market Rally

The Colombo Stock Exchange (CSE) witnessed a strong trading session today with the All Share Price Index (ASPI) gaining **45.23 points (0.40%)** to close at **11,245.67**. The S&P SL20 index declined marginally by 12.45 points (-0.36%) to settle at 3,456.89.

## Banking Sector Leads Gains

The banking sector emerged as the top performer of the session, with **Hatton National Bank (HNB)** rising **1.93%** to LKR 145.00 and **John Keells Holdings (JKH)** gaining **1.92%** to close at LKR 185.50. The sector's strong performance was attributed to improved quarterly earnings expectations and favorable monetary policy outlook.

**Commercial Bank (COMB)** was the only major banking counter to decline, falling **1.88%** to LKR 78.25, following profit-taking after recent gains.

## Market Turnover and Foreign Activity

Total market turnover reached **LKR 2.5 billion**, with foreign investors emerging as net buyers for the third consecutive session, purchasing **LKR 125 million** worth of equities. This sustained foreign buying interest has been a key driver of market sentiment in recent weeks.
